About GoFast

Our Mission

GoFast exists to help you train smarter and PR faster. We give you a personalized training plan, help you discover local runs, and connect you with other runners chasing the same goals.

What We Do

  • Build personalized training plans around your goals
  • Discover local runs and join other people's training
  • Sync with Garmin to track progress and send workouts to your watch
  • Help you achieve your personal records
