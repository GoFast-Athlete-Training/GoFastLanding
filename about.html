About GoFast

Our Mission

GoFast exists to foster the accountability layer so you can PR. We connect runners, build crews, and help you reach your goals through community and support.

What We Do

  • Connect runners with similar pace and goals
  • Build running crews for accountability
  • Provide training support and tracking
  • Help you achieve your personal records
