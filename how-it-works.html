How It Works

1. Find Your Crew

We built custom search to match you with crews that actually fit. No endless scrolling. No guessing if you can keep up.

Search by What Matters

  • • Pace range — Find crews running your speed, from 7-minute miles to comfortable jogging
  • • Location — Search by city, state, or proximity to find crews near you
  • • Running preferences — Road, trail, distance, or training focus
  • • Crew activity — See how often crews actually run, not just what they say

Our search filters out the noise. You see crews you can actually join. Crews where you'll actually fit in. Crews where the pace matches what you can do, not what you aspire to.

2. Show Up Together

Once you join a crew, you're in. You see scheduled runs. You RSVP. You show up.

No complicated onboarding. No secret handshakes. Just join, see the next run, and show up. The crew handles the rest.

3. Consistency Builds Speed — PRs Follow

The magic isn't in the first run. It's in run five, ten, twenty. When showing up becomes automatic. When you stop negotiating with yourself about whether to go.

That's when consistency kicks in. That's when you start seeing progress. That's when PRs happen. Not from the perfect plan. From showing up, week after week, with people who expect you.
